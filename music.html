"Where words fail, Music speaks"
From smoky blues clubs, where you can hear B.B. King using his signature vibrato, to green fields of EDM-festival, where Da Tweekaz hypes the crowd to the tempo of 150 beats-per-min. From heavy metal, where Disturbed showcases their vocal flexibility, to classical music, where you are left in awe by The Devil's Violinist Paganini .

The finite amount of scenarios, emotions, thoughts and events music has, and will continue to put forth.

I practically came out of womb with headphones. My late father always told that those were made by sony, but i honestly doubt authenticity of that story. I bet that those were Harman Kardons. Anyways, i love music. These days i spend my free time bashing my head to piano, since my fingers do not comply as i'd like them to. Guitar, same story. But what i like more than to bash one instrument is.. to bash them all.. at the same time.. Inspired by Hans Zimmer and Two Steps From Hell , i started to get drawn to composing pieces, or atleast trying to. Thats when i bought my first DAW, and started to meddle with music in digital environment. You can check out one of my first compositions below. I recommend using headphones.
