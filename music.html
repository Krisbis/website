"Where words fail, Music speaks"
From smoky blues clubs, where you can hear B.B. King using his signature vibrato, to green fields of EDM-festival, where Da Tweekaz hypes the crowd to the tempo of 150 beats-per-min. From heavy metal, where Disturbed showcases their vocal flexibility, to classical music, where you are left in awe by The Devil's Violinist Paganini .

The finite amount of scenarios, emotions, thoughts and events music has, and will continue to put forth.

I have always been fascinated by sounds. Group them together and they do stuff, like form into harmony, chord or discord. Couple of notes make a question, couple of notes answer back to them. Together they are a scale. I dream of playing some instrument with mastery, and aim at some point take piano classes. Currently i'm self-taught on both guitar, and piano. In other words, i bash my head to the piano, since my fingers do not comply as i'd like them to. But what i like more than to bash one instrument is to bash them all. At the same time. Inspired by Hans Zimmer and Two Steps From Hell , i started to get drawn to composing pieces, or atleast trying to. Now i don't need motor skills of a professional player, and i get to spend any amount of time to create pieces. And so i started to meddle with music in digital environment.
