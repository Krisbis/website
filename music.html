"Where words fail, Music speaks"
From the smoky blues clubs, where the legendary B.B. King captivates audiences with his signature vibrato, to the sun-kissed fields of EDM festivals, where Da Tweekaz pumps up the crowd with the energetic beat of 150bpm. From the thunderous heavy metal, where Disturbed proves that a little bit of headbanging never killed anyone, to the elegant world of classical music, where one is left in awe by the virtuosity of Paganini , the Devil's Violinist.

The seemingly endless array of emotions, thoughts, and experiences that music has and will continue to evoke are truly captivating.

I have always been enamored with the sounds of music. The way that individual sounds come together to form harmonies, chords, and even disharmony, is truly magical. A few notes can make you question your existence, while a few more can provide the answers, or just confuse you even more. Together, they form a scale, which is just a fancy word for a musical rollercoaster. My dream is to one day master an instrument, hopefully without setting it on fire in the process, and I hope to take piano lessons in the future. For now, I am self-taught on both guitar and piano. Though my attempts on the latter are akin to a cat trying to type on a keyboard, I find joy in the struggle to improve. But what I love most is the ability to create music with multiple instruments at once. Inspired by the likes of Hans Zimmer and Two Steps From Hell , I have begun to dabble in composing my own pieces. With the aid of technology, I am able to craft each note and instrument with precision, spending as much time as I need to bring my creations to life.
